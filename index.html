About

I've spent the last 3 years working within the Chief Digital and Artificial Intelligence Office (CDAO) inside the Department of Defense (DoD) for the Defense Digital Service (DDS) alongside software engineers, data scientists, and product managers where I have been designing products and consulting on a range of projects that fall within my organizations mandate to improve technology within the DoD. Before joining the federal workforce I worked for both 0-1 start-ups and large corporations.

Through it all I have been challenged to grow as a designer, manager, and teammate. I lead with empathy to drive results through user-centric design while championing growth within my teams. In a previous life I seriously pursued art and photography having both been published and exhibited.
