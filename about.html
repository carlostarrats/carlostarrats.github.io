Thanks for stopping by. I'm a multidisciplinary Designer based in Los Angeles, California. I specialize and have experience across all the design disciplines (Product, UI, UX, Visual, Brand). Sounds crazy but yeah, I really have done it all. I'm always striving to grow and learn something new and I don't take myself too seriously.

I've been designing since 2002. I lead with empathy to drive design impact while championing growth in team culture. Most recently I have been working to make art more accessible, while in the past I worked at Eloquii (acquired by Walmart) where I was responsible for the creation from concept through deployment for every product decision across desktop, mobile, and app.

In a previous life I seriously pursued art, having had representation as well as gallery shows in Baltimore, Atlanta, Santa Fe, Los Angeles, New York, and the Netherlands.
