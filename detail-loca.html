The Problem

Buying art can be an intimidating experience while barriers exist for artists in both showing their work and selling it.

The Goal

How might we lower barriers for artists bringing more visibility to their work while making it easy to purchase.

The Why

  • For artists: easily connect with spaces interested in them.
  • For spaces: support local artists and add value to their environment.
  • For buyers: support local artists and easily purchase outside of the gallery environment.

Solution

Implement a process that connects users in spaces directly with product through a quick, direct, cost-effective and scaleable process by the use of QR codes.
