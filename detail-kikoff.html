Kikoff Product Discovery

↓

Kikoff is dedicated to helping users build credit.

The Problem

Users are dropping-off at the ebook store on their path to activation. This represents over 40% of the total in-funnel drop-off. Users are not engaging with the store beyond the initial sign-up.

The Goal

How might we offer products of value to our customers while increasing engagement and follow-through.

Customer Research Through Interviews

Do customers learn from and do they use the ebooks they purchase?

  • Time is a problem: users don't have time to sit down and read.
  • Users find the ebooks boring: the content is not engaging enough to hold their attention or retain.
  • Users want the answers: users want relevant answers when they need them. Solutions vs. knowledge
  • Users want progress: reading a book does not change anything. They want more gratification.

Solution

Develop an interactive learning solution that has relevant information targeted to the specific user's need.

User Story

As someone with bad credit, I want to improve my credit so that someday I will be able to buy a home (goal is never just better credit, it's better credit for a purpose: credit = personal finance).

Key Solution Elements

  • Relevancy: a product that offers relevancy to a wide range of users.
  • Repeat engagement: a product that offers newness beyond it's initial offering.
  • Cross-pollination: a product that offers opportunity to grow across other upcoming business verticals.

  • Concern over the structured nature of the experience: what if you have a question outside of what is available?

Business Impact

At this point we had 3 refined directions with variance being in product framing. Due to some complications with the apple store a business decision was made to pause the project. Even-though it was not seen to final, the work done verified proof-of-concept, but at significant investment.

Things to look at: further refinement of the framing in how we want to position the product, are there ways to take the existing chat interaction and make it more free flowing, and development of the visual system.
