The Problem

Users are dropping-off at the ebook store on their path to activation. This represents over 40% of the total in-funnel drop-off. Users are not engaging with the store beyond the initial sign-up.

The Goal

How might we offer products of value to our customers while increasing product engagement.

Customer Research Through Interviews

  • Time is a problem: users don't have time to sit down and read.
  • Users find the ebooks boring: the content is not engaging enough to hold their attention.
  • Users want answers: users want relevant answers when they need them. Solutions vs. knowledge
  • Users want progress: reading a book does not produce results. They want more gratification.

Solution

Develop an interactive learning solution that has relevant information targeted to the specific user's need.

Key Solution Elements

  • Relevancy: a product that offers relevancy to a wide range of users.
  • Repeat engagement: a product that offers newness beyond it's initial offering.
  • Cross-pollination: a product that offers opportunity to grow across other business verticals.
