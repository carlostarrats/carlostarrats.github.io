Sourceability allows customers to operate in and navigate the electronic component market.

The Problem

Users without an account are bouncing at login after submitting a Request for Quote (RFQ) from the PDP.

Background

RFQ's are product/price requests submitted from a product page when stock is unavailable or the price doesn't meet a users expectations. They are pretty common within the electronics sourcing industry.

The Goal

How might we develop a RFQ submission flow that decreases the guest user bounce rate.

Data From Site Analytics

  • On average there are 120 RFQ's submitted per week.
  • On average 80 of them are submitted by logged out users.
  • Of the 80 who submit, 40 bounce immediately at login.

That's 40 potential RFQ's per week (RFQ's have an average value of $1,500) and up to 3 Million $ per year lost.

Solution

Develop a revised RFQ submission flow that accounts for users who have an account as well as guest users.
