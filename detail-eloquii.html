After - Homepage ↓

To better illustrate categories and put business highlighted product out front with a direct click-through, flexible images slots were introduced within a new drop-down desktop nav. The slots can hold one horizontal image or 3 vertical.

After - Menu ↓

Sales and shipping are a big point of focus for conversion for the brand. As such we established a flexible slot controlled by the business team. The slot is able to hold 3, 2, or 1 "box" with the 1 being a rotating message of up to 3 messages if desired. Mobile and tablet automatically default to the 1 box rotating messages regardless of the desktop experience. In addition, on scroll the slot is sticky across desktop, tablet, and mobile for full visibility site-wide to drive urgency.
