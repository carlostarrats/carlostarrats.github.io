The Problem

Previous website limited businesses ability to service the customer timely & effectively while not aligning to the brand mission, which was: fashion is transformative.

The Goal

How might we provide an efficient and enjoyable shopping experience (website + IOS app) that both builds trust with new customers and is reflective of ELOQUII as a company.

Before ↓

Initial Research

Customers were scrolling down the page but were not clicking through toward the end of the header slider. The drop-off past the first image was significant with the following image / marketing not having visibility.

Business Concerns

  • "We would love more flexibility and ownership; making small changes requires too many teams"
  • "The site doesn’t look fashionable"
  • "Communicating who we are to the customer is challenging with so little real estate"

Solution

Develop a design that is modular and easily editable with an increased number of assets, a mobile friendly experience, and a UI specific to the brand that appeals to a range of select users.

Key Solution Elements

  • Flexibility: allow for business to easily edit relevant business propositions and manage assets without design and dev.
  • Imagery: expand the content and the businesses ability to communicate with the customer allowing the clothing more visibility.
  • A clean UI: a visual system that aligns to the brand DNA while also being accessible to customers across a range of specific personas.

Refinements

After - Homepage ↓

To better illustrate categories and put business highlighted product out front with a direct click-through, flexible images slots were introduced within a new drop-down desktop nav. The slots can hold one horizontal image or 3 vertical.

After - Menu ↓

Sales and shipping are a big point of focus for conversion for the brand. As such we established a flexible slot controlled by the business team. The slot is able to hold 3, 2, or 1 "box" with the 1 being a rotating message of up to 3 messages if desired. Mobile and tablet automatically default to the 1 box rotating messages regardless of the desktop experience. In addition, on scroll the slot is sticky across desktop, tablet, and mobile for full visibility site-wide to drive urgency.

After - Messaging Slots ↓

The entire site was re-designed for business flexibility with improved UX, clarity, and brand messaging.

Business Impact

In the end, by building out a more flexible page it allowed business to react in real time by adjusting creative and messaging as needed while improving team wide productivity, providing users with a more streamlined user experience which led to increased customer click-through, conversion, and improved accessibility.
